Information technology and related skills are the subject of the ICT strand, one of the TVL track's strands.

The ICT strand equips students with knowledge and abilities in computer systems servicing, web design, programming,animation, and other related domains.

The TVL track helps students by giving them practical vocational training, preparing them for work after high school, and equipping them with applicable skills for vocations in less time.
